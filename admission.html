About Admission at Rays of Grace Junior School

At Rays of Grace, we welcome learners from diverse backgrounds into our nurturing environment. Our admission process ensures that every student admitted meets our academic, social, and ethical standards.

Requrements

1. Completed application form

2. Birth certificate (copy)

3. Previous school report

4. 2 passport-size photos

5. Medical report

6. Photocopy of Parents National ID

7. Parents Contact Information

8. Parents Place of residence

9. LIN (Learners Identification Number)

Admission Criteria

Admission is based on age appropriateness, academic readiness, and availability of space. Entrance interviews will be conducted to assess your Child's suitability.
