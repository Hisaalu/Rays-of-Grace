Rays of Grace Junior School Shines in 2025 PLE Results

  • 02 Feb 2026
  • System Admin
  • Education

We are thrilled to announce the outstanding performance of our 2025 Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) candidates. True to our motto, "Knowledge Changing Lives Forever," our students have once again demonstrated academic excellence, placing Rays of Grace Junior School in the Second Position in Njeru Municipality.

The 2025 results reflect the hard work, discipline, and resilience of our candidates, as well as the unwavering commitment of our teaching staff. Out of our cohort of 55 candidates, an incredible 72% achieved Division 1 status.

The satistics is: Division 1: 40 Candidates, Division 2: 15 Candidates, Division 3 & 4: 0 Candidates, Failures: 0,

Every single student who sat for the exams at Rays of Grace passed with flying colors, ensuring a 100% transition rate to secondary education

Our students dominated the top aggregate brackets, showcasing the depth of talent at our school. Notable highlights from the aggregate distribution include:

  • 4 Candidates scored Aggregate 05
  • 5 Candidates scored Aggregate 06
  • 3 Candidates scored Aggregate 07
  • 6 Candidates scored Aggregate 08


As we celebrate this victory, registration for new students is currently in progress. We invite you to bring your child to an environment where academic excellence is a tradition.
