Electronic Learning (E-Learning)

A mode of delivering or making content accessible to learners via the web or online (Online Learning), computer networks, CD/DVD, mobile phones, and other electronic technologies. E-Learning expands student convenience, access to materials, peer collaboration, and lesson engagement, building skills for technology-oriented workspaces.

ROGELE Platform LMS

Rays of Grace Online Learning Environment (ROGELE) is our dedicated Learning Environment Platform where teachers upload learning materials, interactive assignments, quizzes, and track student progress. Access ROGELE to join virtual classrooms, download resources, collaborate with peers, and submit assignments online.

Open, Distance & E-Learning

At Rays of Grace Junior School, we embrace flexible learning modalities. Through the Distance Learning Unit, we operationalize Open and Distance eLearning, providing resources, training, and support for both students and facilitators to ensure quality online education.
