About us

Rays of Grace Junior School

Rays of Grace is a registered Mixed Day and Boarding Primary School in Njeru, Buikwe District, Uganda. Founded in 2015 (originally as Mountain of Olives Primary School) and renamed in 2018, the school is built on the motto "Knowledge Changing Lives Forever".

Beyond academics, the school integrates programs like Music, Computer and digital iteracy, and sports easpecially through it's wider Rays of Grace Academy. The school is also known for supporting vulnerable and disadvantaged children through bursaries.

Meet Our Team

The staff of Rays of Grace Junior School is a dedicated, professional and mission driven team commited to providing a holistic learning expereience for every child. They include qualified teachers, support staff and administrators who work together to maintan the high academic standards.

Beyond classroom teaching, the staff plays an active role in shaping learner's character, discplne and social skills. We support extracurricular activities such as music, sports and computer literacy.
