Nursery Graduation Party

28 November 2025 08:30am - 4:00pm Rays of Grace Junior School

Rays of Grace Junior School will proudly host its Top Class Nursery Graduation, a special occasion that celebrates the achievements and growth of its youngest learners as they complete their foundational early education. The event promises to be a joyful and memorable ceremony, bringing together students, teachers, and parents to honor the hard work, creativity, and milestones of the graduating children.
